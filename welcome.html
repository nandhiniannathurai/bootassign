Welcome Coffee

Are you new to Geneva or interested in learning more about the AIWC?  Then we invite you to attend our next monthly Welcome Coffee.  These casual events are a great way to learn about the clubs 40+ activities, special events, excursions and community service projects.  We also serve coffee and home-baked treats.  We look forward to meeting you!

Our Welcome coffees are the second Wednesday of the month (except July and August) from 10:00-12:00 at our Clubrooms.

Directions

We are located at Route de Chene 11, 1207 Geneve, a short block away from the Amandolier bus/tram stop.
